DeAndre Cortez Way (better known as Soulja Boy Tell 'Em or Soulja Boy) [1] [2] (Chicago, Illinois, July 28, 1990 -) is an American rapper, producer and actor. In September 2007, his first single, "Crank That (Soulja Boy)" [3], reached number one on the American Billboard Hot 100 list. The single was self-published on the Internet and later became a chart-topping hit in the United States for seven weeks, beginning in September 2007. [4]
